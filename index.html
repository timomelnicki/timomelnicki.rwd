Start Your own Adventure with the new Crystals for your Home

Crystal of Ice

The shining sapphires the Crystal of Ice is made of, give the impression that the Crystal is molded of eternal ice.
Ice is constructed, light as a feather and massive as a glacier. While fire is unpredictable, ice is controlled and structured. The Crystal is perfect for self-controlling and organized people who want to follow their clear objectives.

Crystal of Fire

Made of rubies but literally seeming constructed by abyssal magma, the Crystal of Fire is perfect for people who don't surrender easily:
Fire isn't just unpredictable, it always finds its way. If it cannot burn through, it burns around. Block it's path and it sends sparks through air, to begin the blaze anew somewhere else

Crystal of Healing

The green emeralds are one of the most valuable gems. Very fitting for the valuable healing powers the Crystal of Healing is representing.
Their importance is often underestimated, so it's the perfect decorative item for every nature fan who wants to show their worth.

Crystal of Darkness

Radiating pure evilness, the Crystal of Darkness is the perfect decorative item for everyone who prefers the dark side.
Molded of Obsidian Stones, the crystal has a beautiful black surface that won't even let the light shine through.
